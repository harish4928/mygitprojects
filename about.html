About Our School

At Nehru International Public School, Noida, we ensure that the students whose future is entrusted to us are given every opportunity to develop into good human beings. We are conscious that no responsibility is of higher magnitude than nurturing and directing a juvenile mind towards the pursuit of excellence.

We encourage our students to develop an open mind with a worldview while not forgetting their Indian heritage and tradition. We would like them to imbibe the values of those who shaped the nation and the ones who are currently leading it. This will give them a 360 ° coverage- Past, Present, and the Future.

As a part of the National Capital Region, Noida has developed into a progressive city with world-class infrastructure. Simultaneously, the need for good educational institutions has also grown. To fulfil this need of the residents, the foundation stone of Nehru International Public School was laid in 2002 in association with JawaharLal Nehru National Youth Center (JNNYC).

Care & Development

  • Care and development of each child with childcentric education.
  • Multiple teachers to see things from a different perspective
  • Identification of the area of interest and nurturing it accordingly.

Teaching Methodology

  • Making learning an enjoyable experience through creative fun.
  • Circle time activities.
  • Magic wand activities.
  • Theme based learning (TBL) – a highly successful progressive learning model.

Events & Celebrations

  • Independence day
  • Earth day
  • Friendship day
  • International Yoga day & More…

Nestling – Kindergarten

In this section we emphasize on child-centric education for the holistic development of each child through interactive methods of creativity and joy in a kindred atmosphere. The children are nurtured by providing emotional support and identifying their areas of interest. An ongoing assessment to provide a stress-free environment.

Elementary School – Primary wing

Creating a culture of evergrowing mindsets, by encouraging and praising efforts than performance; emphasize on the importance of “exercising” their brains; promoting and providing challenges; and focusing on continuous growth with personality development and life skills.

Intermediate – Middle Wing

Activities over here stimulate growth and encourage multi-disciplinary learning, also provides opportunities to recognize their interests and harness the skills through curricular and extracurricular activities with positive moral outcome.

Senior Wing

Here, students get opportunities to acquire necessary knowledge, skills and attitudes for the development of the self and the nation.
