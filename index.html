Best Infrastructure & Experienced Teachers

Noida International Public school (NIPS) Noida, established in 1999, has constantly been progressing to achieve the desired excellence in implementation of teaching and learning practices. The school is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary education and managed by Ch. Shiv Charan Singh Memorial Shiksha Samiti, and caters to the needs of not only the residents of Noida, but also the neighbouring colonies of Ghaziabad.
